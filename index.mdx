## Build AI Agents That Work for Your Business

Weya AI is an end-to-end platform for creating, deploying, and managing AI agents across multiple communication channels.

Whether you're building a customer support assistant, sales representative, appointment scheduler, debt collection agent, or outbound calling campaign, Weya AI provides everything you need—from agent creation to campaign analytics—in one place.

With Weya AI you can:

- 🤖 Build Voice, Video, and WhatsApp AI Agents
- 📚 Connect Knowledge Bases to provide accurate responses
- 📞 Purchase and manage phone numbers
- 📢 Launch large-scale outbound calling campaigns
- 🔗 Integrate with CRMs, APIs, and custom tools
- 🪝 Receive real-time events using Webhooks
- 📊 Track calls, conversations, and campaign performance
- 🌍 Deploy multilingual AI agents for global audiences
