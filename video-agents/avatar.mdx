# Choose an Avatar

An avatar is the visual representation of your Video Agent during conversations. It provides a face for your AI assistant, making interactions feel more natural and engaging.

Selecting the right avatar helps reinforce your brand identity and creates a better customer experience.

---

## Why Avatars Matter

A well-designed avatar can:

- Improve customer engagement
- Create a more human-like interaction
- Build trust during conversations
- Represent your brand professionally
- Enhance onboarding and support experiences

---

## Selecting an Avatar

When creating a Video Agent:

1. Navigate to **Video Agents**.
2. Open an existing Video Agent or create a new one.
3. Locate the **Avatar** section.
4. Browse the available avatars.
5. Select the avatar that best matches your use case.
6. Save your changes.

---

## Choosing the Right Avatar

Consider the following when selecting an avatar:

| Use Case | Recommended Style |
|----------|-------------------|
| Customer Support | Friendly and approachable |
| Sales | Professional and confident |
| Healthcare | Calm and trustworthy |
| Education | Clear and engaging |
| Banking & Finance | Professional and formal |
| Hospitality | Warm and welcoming |

---

## Match Your Brand

Choose an avatar that aligns with your organization's identity.

For example:

- Professional appearance for enterprise businesses
- Friendly appearance for customer service
- Modern appearance for technology companies
- Formal appearance for financial institutions

Maintaining consistency between your avatar, voice, and conversation style creates a more natural user experience.

---

## Pair the Avatar with the Right Voice

Your avatar should complement the selected voice.

A good combination includes:

- Matching language
- Appropriate speaking style
- Consistent personality
- Suitable conversation tone

## Best Practices

- Choose an avatar that represents your business professionally.
- Use the same avatar across customer interactions for consistency.
- Match the avatar with your brand personality.
- Select a voice that complements the avatar.
- Test conversations before deploying the agent.
