The Dashboard provides a real-time overview of your AI agent's performance and call activities. It helps you track usage, analyze call outcomes, and monitor your concurrency limits.

![Dashboard Overview](/images/dashboard/dashboard.png)

## Key Metrics

### Total Calls
Shows the total number of calls made and received across all your agents.
- **Inbound**: Calls received by your agents.
- **Outbound**: Calls initiated by your agents (e.g., via campaigns).

### Minute Usage
Tracks the total duration of calls in minutes. This is crucial for monitoring your billing and usage limits.
- **Inbound Minutes**: Duration of incoming calls.
- **Outbound Minutes**: Duration of outgoing calls.

### Concurrency
Displays the number of simultaneous calls currently active compared to your limit.
- **Available Channels**: Your maximum allowed concurrent calls.

## Analytics & Charts

### Usage Analytics
A visual representation of your call volume over time, helping you identify peak usage periods.

### Call Pickup Rate
The percentage of outbound calls that were answered by recipients. diverse pickup rates can indicate the quality of your lead list or the timing of your calls.

### Call Status Distribution
Breaks down calls by their final status (e.g., Completed, Failed, Busy, No Answer).

### Call Transfer Rate
The percentage of calls that were transferred to a human agent or another destination.

### Call Disconnect Reason
Insights into why calls ended (e.g., Agent Ended, Customer Ended, Error). This helps in troubleshooting and optimizing agent behavior.

### Call Outcomes
Categorizes the results of your calls based on your specific business goals (e.g., Appointment Set, Sale Made, Not Interested).

## Filters
You can filter all data on the dashboard by **Date Range** using the picker at the top right. This allows you to view daily, weekly, or monthly performance.
