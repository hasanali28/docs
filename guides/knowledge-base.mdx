The Knowledge Base is a central repository where you can upload documents that your agents can reference during conversations. This allows them to answer specific questions about your business, products, or policies.

![Knowledge Base](/images/kb/kb.png)

## Adding a Knowledge Base

1. Navigate to the **Knowledge Base** page.
2. Click **Add Knowledge Base**.
3. provide a **Name** and **Description**.
4. Upload your documents (Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOCX).
5. Click **Save**.

## Using a Knowledge Base

Once created, you must attach the Knowledge Base to an agent:
1. Go to **Agents**.
2. Select an agent to edit.
3. Navigate to the **Knowledge Base & Tools** tab.
4. Select your Knowledge Base from the list.
![Knowledge Base](/images/kb/kb_use.png)
5. **Save** the agent configuration.

Your agent will now use the information in the uploaded documents to generate more accurate responses.
