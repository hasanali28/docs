The **Phone Numbers** page allows you to purchase, import, and manage the phone numbers your agents use.

![Phone Numbers](/images/phone/phone_list.png)

## Adding a Number

You can add numbers from supported providers like **Twilio**, **Plivo**, or **Telnyx**.

1. Click **Add Number** and select **Add Phone Number**.
2. Choose your provider (e.g., Twilio).
3. Enter the **Phone Number**, **Account SID**, and **Auth Token**.
4. Click **Add**.

## Assigning to an Agent

To make a number active, you must assign it to an agent:
1. Find the number in the list.
2. Click the **Edit** (pencil) icon or use the **Assign** action.
3. Select whether it's for **Inbound** or **Outbound** calls.
4. Choose the **Agent** that should handle calls on this number.

## WhatsApp Integration

You can also connect WhatsApp numbers to enable text-based interactions alongside voice calls.
1. Click **Add Number** > **Add WhatsApp Number**.
2. Provide the required credentials (Phone Number ID, Access Token).
3. Assign a WhatsApp-capable agent.
