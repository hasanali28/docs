# Quickstart

Welcome to Weya AI! This guide walks you through creating your first AI-powered solution—from building an agent to launching a live campaign.

By the end of this guide, you'll have:

- 🤖 An AI Agent ready to answer customers
- 📚 A Knowledge Base connected to your agent
- 📞 A phone number linked to your workspace
- 📢 A campaign ready to make or receive calls
- 📊 Access to analytics, call recordings, and transcripts

Make sure you have:

- A Weya AI account

- A verified email address
- Internet access

- Email & Password
- Google
