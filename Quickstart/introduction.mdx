# Welcome to Weya AI

Welcome to **Weya AI**, an enterprise platform for building, deploying, and managing AI-powered customer interactions across voice, video, and messaging channels.

Whether you're creating an AI receptionist, automating customer support, running outbound campaigns, or engaging customers on WhatsApp, Weya AI provides everything you need in one unified platform.

---

## What can you build?

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Voice Agents" icon="phone">
Build intelligent AI voice agents that can answer inbound calls, make outbound calls, qualify leads, collect payments, schedule appointments, and provide customer support using natural conversations.
</Card>

<Card title="Video Agents" icon="video">
Create AI-powered video agents for interactive customer experiences, personalized engagement, onboarding, product demonstrations, and virtual assistance.
</Card>

<Card title="WhatsApp Agents" icon="message">
Automate customer conversations on WhatsApp using AI. Respond instantly, answer questions, qualify leads, collect information, and integrate with your existing workflows.
</Card>

<Card title="Voice Blaster" icon="bullhorn">
Launch large-scale outbound voice campaigns using AI-generated or pre-recorded voice messages for reminders, promotions, surveys, notifications, and announcements.
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## What You'll Learn

By following this Quickstart guide, you'll learn how to:

- Sign in to the Weya AI Dashboard
- Create your first AI Agent
- Upload a Knowledge Base
- Connect a Phone Number
- Create a Campaign
- Import Contacts
- Launch your first AI-powered campaign
- Monitor calls and analytics

## Before You Begin

Before continuing, make sure you have:

- A Weya AI account
- Access to your Weya AI Dashboard
- A supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari)
- An internet connection
