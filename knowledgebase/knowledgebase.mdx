## Step 1: Create a Knowledge Base

1. Navigate to the **Knowledge Base** tab in your Weya dashboard.  
2. Click **Add KB**.  
3. Enter the details (name, description) and attach any relevant files.  
4. Click **Create** to save your knowledge base.

---

## Step 2: Connect Knowledge Base to an Agent

![Tools & Knowledge Base](/images/kb1.png)

1. Go to the **Agents** section in your dashboard.  
2. Open the **Knowledge Base & Tools** settings for the agent where you want to add a knowledge source.  
3. Choose from the available knowledge bases (e.g., `"KB-1"` or `"KB-2"`) to select which source your agent will use.
