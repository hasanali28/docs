# WhatsApp Templates

WhatsApp Templates are pre-approved message formats used to initiate conversations with customers or send notifications outside the standard customer service window.

Templates ensure that your business communications comply with WhatsApp Business Platform policies while enabling automated messaging through your AI agents.

---

## What Are WhatsApp Templates?

Templates are reusable messages that have been approved by WhatsApp.

They are commonly used for:

- Appointment reminders
- Payment reminders
- Order confirmations
- Delivery updates
- Customer notifications
- Marketing campaigns
- Authentication messages
- Follow-up messages

Once approved, templates can be used by your WhatsApp Agents during automated workflows.

---

## Template Categories

WhatsApp supports different categories of templates depending on the purpose of the message.

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Utility | Transactional messages such as order updates, invoices, reminders, and account notifications. |
| Marketing | Promotional offers, announcements, campaigns, and customer engagement messages. |
| Authentication | One-time passwords (OTPs) and account verification messages. |

<Note>

The available template categories may vary depending on your WhatsApp Business configuration.

</Note>

## Viewing Available Templates

To view your templates:

1. Sign in to the Weya AI Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **WhatsApp**.
3. Open the **Templates** section.
4. Select the connected WhatsApp number.

The dashboard displays all templates associated with the selected WhatsApp Business account.

---

## Template Information

Each template includes important details such as:

- Template Name
- Category
- Language
- Approval Status
- Variables
- Last Updated

This information helps you identify which templates are available for use.

---

## Using Templates with AI Agents

WhatsApp Agents can use approved templates to:

- Start customer conversations
- Send automated follow-up messages
- Deliver appointment reminders
- Notify customers about order status
- Trigger workflow notifications

Templates are automatically populated with customer-specific information using template variables.

## Managing Templates

As your business grows, you can:

- Review existing templates
- Update template content
- Organize templates by purpose
- Use different templates for different workflows

Keeping your templates organized makes automation easier to manage.

---

## Best Practices

- Use descriptive template names.
- Choose the appropriate template category.
- Keep messages short and easy to understand.
- Personalize messages using template variables whenever possible.
- Regularly review templates to ensure they remain relevant.
