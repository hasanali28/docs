# Connect a WhatsApp Number

Before your AI agent can send or receive messages, you must connect a WhatsApp Business number to your Weya AI workspace.

Once connected, you can assign AI agents, send template messages, automate customer conversations, and manage incoming messages from a single dashboard.

---

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

- A Weya AI account
- A WhatsApp Business Account
- A verified phone number
- Administrator access to your Meta Business account

<Note>

Only verified WhatsApp Business numbers can be connected to Weya AI.

</Note>

---

## Step 1: Open WhatsApp Integration

1. Sign in to the Weya AI Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **WhatsApp**.
3. Open the **Integrations** section.
4. Click **Connect WhatsApp Number**.

---

## Step 2: Verify Your WhatsApp Number

Enter your WhatsApp Business phone number and complete the verification process.

During verification, Weya AI checks that:

- The number is valid.
- The number is registered with WhatsApp Business.
- The number is available for integration.

Once verification is complete, continue with the setup.

---

## Step 3: Configure the Integration

Provide the required integration details.

Typical configuration includes:

| Field | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Display Name | Business name shown to customers. |
| Phone Number | Verified WhatsApp Business number. |
| Business Account | Meta Business account associated with the number. |
| Webhook | Endpoint used to receive WhatsApp events. |

After entering the required information, save the integration.

---

## Step 4: Verify the Connection

After connecting your number:

- The integration status should display as **Connected**.
- Your WhatsApp number will appear in the dashboard.
- The number becomes available for AI agent assignment.

## Test the Connection

Before going live:

- Send a test message to your connected number.
- Verify that incoming messages reach your AI agent.
- Confirm that replies are delivered successfully.
- Check that webhooks are receiving events correctly.

Testing ensures your integration is working as expected.

---

## Best Practices

- Use a dedicated WhatsApp Business number for automation.
- Complete the verification process before assigning an AI agent.
- Configure approved message templates for outbound communication.
- Regularly review webhook events and delivery logs.
- Keep your business information updated within Meta Business Manager.

---

## Troubleshooting

If your number cannot be connected:

- Verify that the phone number is registered with WhatsApp Business.
- Confirm that the number is not already linked to another integration.
- Check your Meta Business permissions.
- Ensure the webhook configuration is valid.
- Retry the verification process after resolving any issues.
