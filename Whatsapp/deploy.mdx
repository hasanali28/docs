# Deploy Your WhatsApp Agent

Once your WhatsApp Agent has been configured with its instructions, Knowledge Base, templates, and integrations, it's ready to be deployed.

Deploying your agent allows it to automatically respond to incoming WhatsApp messages and participate in customer conversations using your connected WhatsApp Business number.

---

## Before You Deploy

Before making your agent available to customers, ensure that the following are configured:

- WhatsApp Business number is connected.
- AI Agent is created and configured.
- System Prompt is complete.
- Knowledge Base is connected (recommended).
- Required templates are available.
- Webhook configuration is complete.
- Required tools and integrations are configured.

<Check>

Testing your configuration before deployment helps ensure a smooth customer experience.

</Check>

---

## Deploy Your Agent

To deploy your WhatsApp Agent:

1. Sign in to the **Weya AI Dashboard**.
2. Navigate to **WhatsApp Agents**.
3. Select the agent you want to deploy.
4. Verify your configuration.
5. Assign the agent to a connected WhatsApp Business number.
6. Save your changes.

Once deployed, your agent is ready to receive and respond to incoming WhatsApp messages.

---

## Test the Deployment

Before using your agent in production, send a few test messages.

Verify that:

- Incoming messages are received.
- The AI responds naturally.
- Knowledge Base answers are accurate.
- Templates are sent correctly.
- Variables are populated correctly.
- Custom tools execute successfully.
- CRM integrations work as expected.

Testing helps identify issues before customers interact with your agent.

---

## Monitor Conversations

After deployment, monitor your WhatsApp Agent to ensure it performs as expected.

Review:

- Incoming conversations
- AI responses
- Message delivery status
- Template usage
- Tool execution
- Integration activity
- Conversation history

Regular monitoring helps improve both response quality and customer satisfaction.

---

## Update Your Agent

Your WhatsApp Agent can be updated at any time.

Common updates include:

- Editing the System Prompt
- Updating the Knowledge Base
- Connecting additional tools
- Assigning a different WhatsApp number
- Updating webhook settings
- Using new message templates

Changes can be saved and applied whenever needed.

---

## Best Practices

- Test every workflow before deploying to production.
- Keep your Knowledge Base up to date.
- Use approved WhatsApp Templates for outbound communication.
- Regularly review customer conversations to improve responses.
- Monitor webhook events and integration logs.
- Keep your business information and templates updated.
