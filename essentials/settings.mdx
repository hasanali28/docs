## Overview

Weya is a platform for building, testing, deploying, and monitoring AI-driven campaigns and phone agents.

This guide will walk you through:
1. Creating your account  
2. Building your first phone agent  
3. Connecting a phone number  
4. Launching your first campaign  

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Visit the **weya Dashboard**.  
2. Sign up for a new account.  

![Weya AI Workflow](/images/signup.png)

---

### Step 2: Create New Agent
<video width="800" controls>
  <source src="/images/createagent.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

1. Navigate to the **Agents** tab.  
2. Click Create an Agent and select **Create Phone Agent**.  
3. Choose Agent Category **Single-Prompt Agent**.  
4. Select Call Type **Outbound**.  
5. Click Choose Template and then continue.  
6. Update the **Prompt** and **System Message**.  
7. Click **Save**.  

---

### Step 4: Connect Phone Number
<video width="800" controls>
  <source src="/images/pho.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

1. Go to the **Phone Numbers** tab.  
2. Click **Add Number** → select **Add Phone Number**.  
3. Choose your **service provider**, enter credentials, and click **Save**.  
4. Click **Assign Outbound Agent**.  
5. Select the **agent** you created.  
6. Click **Connect Agent**.  

---

### Step 5: Create Campaign
1. Navigate to the **Campaigns** tab.  
2. Click **Create Batch** → select **Add Phone Number**.  
3. Enter all required details.  
4. Click **Create Campaign**.  

---

## Congratulations!

Your agent is now live and can:  
- Make outbound calls  
- Handle natural conversations automatically  
- Process requests 24/7
