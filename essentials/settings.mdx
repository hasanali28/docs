## Overview

Weya is the all-in-one platform for building, testing, deploying, and monitoring AI-driven campaigns and agents — across **Phone, Video, and WhatsApp** — from a single dashboard.

Unlike single-channel tools, Weya lets you launch a voice agent (including high-volume Voice Blasters), a video agent, and a WhatsApp conversational agent from the same workspace, using the same prompt logic and the same analytics.

This guide will walk you through:

1. Creating your account
2. Choosing your Agent Type (Phone, Voice Blaster, Video, or WhatsApp)
3. Building your agent
4. Connecting your channel (phone number, WhatsApp Business number, or generating a video link)
5. Launching your first campaign
6. Monitoring live performance

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the **Weya Dashboard**.
2. Sign up for a new account.
3. Verify your email and complete your workspace setup.

---

### Step 2: Choose Your Agent Type

Weya supports four agent types out of the box — pick the one that fits your use case, or build several for an omnichannel campaign.

| Agent Type | Best For |
|---|---|
| **Phone Agent** | 1:1 outbound/inbound voice calls, IVR replacement, sales & support calls |
| **Voice Blaster** | High-volume outbound voice broadcasts — **Static** (fixed pre-recorded/scripted message) or **Dynamic** (AI-generated, personalized per contact) |
| **Video Agent** | Face-to-face conversations — currently **Inbound only** |
| **WhatsApp Agent** | Conversational messaging, reminders, order updates, support chat |

1. Navigate to the **Agents** tab.
2. Click **Create an Agent**.
3. Select your **Agent Type**: `Phone Agent`, `Voice Blaster`, `Video Agent`, or `WhatsApp Agent`.

---

### Step 3: Build Your Agent

#### Option A — Phone Agent
1. Choose Agent Category: **Single-Prompt Agent** (or **Multi-Prompt Agent** for complex flows).
2. Select Call Type: **Outbound** or **Inbound**.
3. Click **Choose Template** and continue.
4. Update the **Prompt** and **System Message**.
5. Set voice, language, and interruption sensitivity.
6. Click **Save**.

#### Option B — Voice Blaster
Created from the **Agents** tab just like every other agent type.
1. Select Blaster Type:
   - **Static** — upload or record a fixed message that plays the same way for every contact.
   - **Dynamic** — write a **Prompt** and **System Message** so the AI generates a personalized version of the message per contact (e.g. inserting name, order details, due dates).
2. Set voice, language, and (for Dynamic) response handling for callee interruptions.
3. Click **Save**.

#### Option C — Video Agent
1. Choose an **Avatar** (or upload a custom one) and select a voice to match.
2. Session Type is currently **Inbound only** — contacts join a video link to reach the agent.
3. Click **Choose Template** and continue.
4. Update the **Prompt** and **System Message** — this drives what the avatar says and how it responds on screen.
5. Click **Save**.

#### Option D — WhatsApp Agent
1. Choose Agent Category: **Single-Prompt Agent** or **Flow-Based Agent**.
2. Select Conversation Type: **Outbound Broadcast** or **Inbound Support**.
3. Click **Choose Template** and continue.
4. Update the **Prompt**, **System Message**, and any **Quick Reply buttons/menus**.
5. Click **Save**.

---

### Step 4: Connect Your Channel

#### Phone Number (Phone Agent & Voice Blaster)
Voice Blaster uses the exact same phone connection flow as Phone Agent — no separate setup.
1. Go to the **Phone Numbers** tab.
2. Click **Add Number** → select **Add Phone Number**.
3. Choose your **service provider**, enter credentials, and click **Save**.
4. Click **Assign Outbound Agent** → select your **Phone Agent** or **Voice Blaster** → **Connect Agent**.

#### Video Agent
No separate connection step needed — your **Video Link** is generated automatically when you create the campaign in Step 5.

#### WhatsApp Business Number
1. Go to the **WhatsApp** tab.
2. Click **Add Number** → connect via **WhatsApp Business API** (Meta-verified).
3. Enter your **Business ID** and credentials, then click **Save**.
4. Click **Assign WhatsApp Agent** → select your agent → **Connect Agent**.

---

### Step 5: Create Campaign

1. Navigate to the **Campaigns** tab.
2. Click **Create Campaign** → choose the channel: **Phone**, **Voice Blaster**, **Video**, or **WhatsApp** (or select **Omnichannel** to combine them). Voice Blaster campaigns follow the exact same setup flow as Phone Agent campaigns.
3. **For Video campaigns:** your unique **Video Link** is generated automatically at this step — copy or share it directly with contacts (no separate setup required).
4. Click **Add Contacts** → upload your list or connect a CRM.
5. Enter all required details (schedule, throttling, retry rules).
6. Click **Create Campaign**.

---

### Step 6: Monitor & Optimize

1. Go to the **Analytics** tab to track calls, video sessions, and WhatsApp conversations in real time.
2. Review transcripts, sentiment, and outcome tags per interaction.
3. A/B test prompts across channels without leaving the dashboard.
